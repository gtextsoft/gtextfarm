Revolutionizing the Agricultural Sector – One Harvest at a Time

Gtext Farms is a dynamic subsidiary of Gtext Holdings, committed to transforming Nigeria's agricultural landscape through innovative, sustainable farming practices and efficient direct-to-consumer supply chains. We grow, harvest, process, and deliver premium produce that meets the highest standards of quality, freshness, and nutrition.


By minimizing middlemen, we ensure fair returns for farmers and exceptional value for our customers – whether households, restaurants, hotels, schools, markets, or exporters.


Our mission is clear: Deliver reliable access to superior locally grown food that tastes better, supports health, and strengthens Nigeria's food security and economy.

Fresh, High-Quality Produce Grown with Pride in Nigeria

Our premium selection of farm-fresh products, cultivated with sustainable methods and rigorous quality control.

Premium Parboiled Rice

Stone-free, long-grain rice that swells beautifully, stays fluffy, and delivers rich aroma. Milled fresh for superior nutrition.

Vibrant Fresh Tomatoes

Plump, juicy, and deep red. Harvested at peak ripeness, ideal for stews and sauces. High in lycopene and vitamins.

Spicy & Aromatic Peppers

Bell peppers, scotch bonnet (ata rodo), and chili varieties. Crisp, flavorful, and packed with healthy heat.

Golden Maize (Corn)

Sweet, tender yellow and white grains. Great for pap, tuwo, or roasting. Naturally dried options available.

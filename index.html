From Agriculture to Structured Food Production

Gtext Farms, a subsidiary of Gtext Holdings, operates as a modern food production enterprise that combines primary agriculture with professional processing and distribution systems. Our model is built to support consistent quality, food safety, and dependable supply volumes across Nigeria.


We partner with growers, aggregators, processors, and logistics teams to ensure traceability and efficient product flow. This structure enables us to serve both retail and high-volume institutional demand with confidence.


Our mission: Build trusted food systems that strengthen local production, reduce waste, and deliver nutritious, high-quality products to market at scale.

About The Company

Building Trusted Food Systems Across Nigeria

Gtext Farms is a subsidiary of Gtext Holdings focused on integrated food production, processing, and reliable supply for commercial and institutional markets.

Who We Are

Our organization combines agriculture, post-harvest operations, quality assurance, packaging, and logistics into one professional operating model. We serve a broad demand base, from households to high-volume buyers, while maintaining consistent product standards and transparent communication.


By strengthening local sourcing and reducing inefficiencies in the value chain, we support food security, responsible production, and long-term economic impact.
